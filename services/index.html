Our services

At the Center for Economic Research and Consulting, we work with businesses, government and regulators to help shape strategies.

Consultancy

We provide tailored analysis, reports and advice that help our clients understand and shape their decision making based on economic insights. Our advice enables our cliets to assess the economic impact of their own strategies, operations and policies and as well new government policies coming into effect.

Bepoke Research

At CERC we engage in commissioned reearch on a stand-alone basis. We also design and conduct bespoke surveys and systematic literature reviews to enable important questions regarding efficacy and economic value to be answered. CERC works closely with its clients to define the scope of the research, analyse data from studies, and report these in order to answer our clients' questions and meet their needs.

Workshops and Briefings

Our team members are available to deliver presentations at key stakeholder meetings and conferences. We are available to support the discussion of economic impact, and provide economic reasoning to key stakeholders.
