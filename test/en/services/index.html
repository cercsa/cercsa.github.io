Services

In CERC, we work with businesses, government and regulators to help shape strategies
  • Bepoke Research: At CERC we engage in commissioned work on a stand-alone basis. We also design and conduct bespoke surveys and systematic literature reviews to enable important questions regarding efficacy and economic value to be answered. CERC works closely with its clients to define the scope of the research, analyse data from studies included and report these in order to answer these question.
  • Workshops & Briefings: Our team members are available to deliver presentations at key stakeholder meetings and conferences. We are available to support the discussion of economic case, and provide economic reasoning to key stakeholders.
