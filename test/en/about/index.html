Who we are

CERC was established by Prof. Nourah A Alyousef with the ambition to produce domestic economics with impact. CERC aligns its activities with the objectives of Vision 2030 through plans to study the proposed decisions and approved implementation plans that are guided by pre-defined goals and key performance indicators. to support the aspirations and capabilities of our country to achieve their fullest potential. CERC draws upon and harness knowledge of experts in the advanced modern econometrics, artificial intelligence, data science. The center brings together a distinguished team that includes a number of graduates of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques Program, from the best international universities. They play a vital role in our forward thinking and vision as they are part of the important transformation journey incSaudi Arabia across various sectors.

Our approach

As big data and data analytics are increasingly integrated across sectors, this creates a platform for data-driven policy analysis and evalutaion that is based on precise, continuous, dynamic data, generated from observed behaviour.

The centre's approach is to build on and utilize advanced scientific foundations from more traditional econometric analysis tools to state-of-the-art models and machine learning algorithms for economic analysis (including but not limited to measuring and evaluating the impact of economic decisions and developed system).

The centre offers a number of services including bespoke research to meet our clients needs. Our work ranges from producing preliminary reports for feasibility studies to in depth reports that can inform policy and project decision making.

In addition, CERC preform consultations on proposed regulations, as well economic domestic and international issues.

The center also publishes collections of economic and policy reports on a variety of economic and technical aspects synthesized by leading experts in the fields of economic, data science and artificial intelligence.

The centre also serves its clients through dedicated events (such as conference engagements, workshops, and symposiums).
