About

Vision

To play a vital role in supporting economic policy assessments.

We enable policymakers and the public to make informed decisions about public policy issues.

In addition, preform consultations on proposed Regulations, Economic domestic and international issues.

Role of the Centre

The Centre for Economic Research and Consultancy aligns its activities with the objectives of Vision 2030 through plans to study the proposed decisions and approved implementation plans that are guided by pre-defined goals and key performance indicators.

This is in order to achieve and deepen the impact and involve a distinguished team that includes a number of graduates of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques Program, from the best international universities. This is because they are part of the important transformation journey in our dear country to bring about a qualitative shift in various sectors, which was accompanied by the development of new programs that require decisions to reflect the aspirations and capabilities of our country to the fullest.

With the rapid development of econometric analysis tools and its developments in line with technical progress and the use of advanced artificial intelligence methods, which enabled the adoption of valuable and highly efficient models. The centre's approach is to build on advanced scientific foundations (from more traditional methods to state-of-the-art models and machine learning algorithms) for economic analysis (including but not limited to measuring and evaluating the impact of economic decisions and developed system).

The centre offers a number of services including bespoke research to meet our clients needs. Our work ranges from producing preliminary reports for feasibility and proof of concept projects to in depth reports that can inform policy and project decision making.

The Center also publishes collections of economic and policy reports on a variety of economic and technical aspects synthesized by leading experts in the fields of economic, data science and artificial intelligence.

The centre also serves its clients through dedicated Economic Reports, and events (such as conference engagements, Workshops, and Symposiums).
