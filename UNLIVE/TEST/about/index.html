What we do

The Center for Economic Research and Consulting was established by Professor Nourah Alyousef with the ambition of conducting impactful research and consultancy for the Saudi Arabian economy.

CERC helps organisations align their activities with the objectives of Vision 2030. We provide government and non-governmental agencies with an analysis of the possible economic effects of their proposed implementations of Vision 2030 aims.

In addition, we conduct research and bespoke consultancy on domestic and international economic themes affecting the Saudi economy.

In so doing, we support the aspirations and capabilities of our country to achieve its fullest economic potential.

Our approach

With the profusion of big data across sectors, and the development of ever-more sophisticated econometrics and machine learning, the opportunity arises for a data-driven policy analysis and evalutaion that is based on precise, continuous, dynamic data, generated from observed behaviour.

CERC seizes this opportunity. Our experts use tools from advanced econometrics and artificial intelligence for economic analysis—including, but not limited to, measuring and evaluating the impact of economic decisions.

In doing this, we also draw on the confirmed experience of our experts and advisors, in their years of work on Saudi Arabian economics. CERC is both state-of-the-art and firmly grounded in local expertise and our profound sense of duty to Saudi Arabia and its people.
